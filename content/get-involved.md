## Pledge to vote

The simplest, most powerful thing you can do is commit — and then bring one
friend. Reply to any of our reminders with "in" and we'll keep you on track for
every election you're eligible for.

## Invite us to your community event

Hosting a Community event? We would love to attend!

We are interested in community events where we can encourage people to register,
be aware of upcoming elections and sign up for reminders.

If you know of any such events, please let us know!

## Volunteering

Interested in voluntering? Right now we want to build a pool of interested
volunteers who are interested in going door-to-door to make the community
aware of an upcoming election or encouraging registration.

Volunteers must be:
- **Confident and Bold!** — Be willing to go door-to-door
- **Dedicated to the Cause** — Passionate about voter turnout

As election cycles come closer, we will reach out to you for a targeted neighborhood
or region.

> **Note for contributors:** we're required to collect your name, mailing
> address, and (for individuals) occupation and employer once your contributions
> total more than $100 in a calendar year. Gathering it up front saves everyone
> time later.

Ready? Head to [Contact](/contact/) and tell us how you'd like to help.
