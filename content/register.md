## Are you registered?

Before anything else, check your status. Virginia's registration rolls are
updated constantly, so even longtime voters can fall off after a move or a
missed election cycle.

[Check your registration status](https://vote.elections.virginia.gov/VoterInformation)

## Register for the first time

Virginia lets you register online, by mail, or in person. Online is the
fastest — you'll need your Virginia driver's license or DMV ID number.

[Register online at vote.va.gov](https://vote.elections.virginia.gov/Registration/Eligibility)

**Deadlines matter.** Registration closes 15 days before Election Day for
online and mail registrations. In-person registration at your local general
registrar's office closes at the end of business on the deadline date.

## Update your registration

Moved recently? Changed your name? Update your registration so your polling
place assignment is correct.

[Update your registration](https://vote.elections.virginia.gov/Registration/Eligibility)

## Registered?

If you have successfully registered, are you aware of the upcoming elections? You can sign up for
election reminders.
