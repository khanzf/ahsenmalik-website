## Get election reminders

The most common reason people miss elections is simple: they forgot. We send
short, plain-text reminders before every federal, state, and local election in
Northern Virginia — no spam, no fundraising asks, just the date and the key
information you need.

[Sign up for reminders](/get-involved/)

## Bring a friend

Turnout multiplies when people go together. Tell one person — a neighbor, a
coworker, a family member — and offer to go with them. That single conversation
is the highest-impact thing any volunteer can do.

## Get a ride

No car? Work schedule too tight? We coordinate volunteer drivers for Election
Day and early voting periods. Rides are free and available across Fairfax,
Loudoun, Prince William, and Arlington.

[Request a ride](/contact/)

## Volunteer as a driver or reminder caller

If you have a car and a few free hours around Election Day, or if you're
comfortable making friendly reminder calls, we want to hear from you.

[Get involved](/get-involved/)

## Why it matters

Statewide and local races in Virginia routinely come down to a few hundred
votes — sometimes fewer. Every single person who shows up changes the outcome.
That's not a slogan; it's math.
